FAQ

Why do I see the tracking permission prompt?

To comply with iOS privacy rules and provide a personalized experience, the app requests App Tracking Transparency on first launch. You can allow or deny; denying does not affect core features.

Family controls and shielding

The app can integrate with system family controls. To adjust shielding, use settings in the app and share your needs with us.
